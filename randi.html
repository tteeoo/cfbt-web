Free DART Pass for UT Dallas Students

If you are UT Dallas student and have a valid Comet Card, are registered and have paid the fees for the current semester, you can get a semester-long pass to use all of DART's public transportation for free.

To apply, visit the UTD page to request a DART GoPass and fill out the form including your NetID and password (the same as to log in to eLearning). Then, download DART's GoPass app on the App Store or Google Play. Your pass should automatically appear in the app within a week. When asked for a ticket on a DART bus or train, simply open the app and your pass.

Physical passes can also be acquired by contacting park@utdallas.edu. UTD faculty and staff can also acquire free pass through a separate form.
